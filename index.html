Frequently asked questions

Is MiraVisa a law firm?
No. MiraVisa is a technology platform and does not provide legal advice. Our flows and checks were designed with input from experienced immigration professionals.
Do I pay per application?
Yes. Pricing is per application. No subscriptions or hidden fees.
How reliable are the AI checks?
Expert AI runs consistency checks, risk flags, and confidence scoring in real time. It improves quality but does not replace an immigration officer or a lawyer.
Can I get human help?
Yes. You can choose a plan with human review for added reassurance or consult an attorney separately for complex cases.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data. It is only used to support your application.
